Who We Are

A Palestinian non-profit dedicated to fostering peace and empowerment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ignite a global movement of empowered women and youth who champion peace, nonviolence, and democratic values. Through innovative education, bold advocacy, and grassroots community engagement, we aim to transform conflict zones into thriving hubs of sustainable development. We envision a world where every voice resonates, where human rights are the cornerstone of society, and where the power of nonviolent action dismantles the foundations of discrimination and injustice.

Our Vision

We dare to imagine a world where the energy of youth and the wisdom of women converge to rewrite the narrative of peace. Our vision is a tapestry of resilient communities, woven together by threads of mutual understanding, where every individual is an architect of positive change. We see a future where peace is not just the absence of conflict, but a vibrant ecosystem of justice, equality, and shared prosperity, nurtured by the hands of empowered women and youth who lead with compassion, innovate with purpose, and govern with integrity.

Our Partners

Collaboration is at the heart of our success. We are proud to work with a diverse network of local and international organizations, as well as actors from the private sector. These partnerships amplify our impact, allowing us to leverage shared knowledge and resources to better serve Palestinian women and youth. Together, we are stronger and more effective in our mission to build a peaceful and prosperous future.
