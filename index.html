For Active Participation and Social Empowerment

Taqadum is a non-profit Palestinian organization, founded in 2022, aiming to create a positive impact for Palestinian women and youth in public affairs, through participation, influence, and constructive contribution. It aims to break free from ready-made social molds and canned ideas by leaving the comfortable societal margins towards horizons that unleash and empower the energies of Palestinian youth through initiative, criticism, creativity, and effective contribution to the public sphere. The organization aims to motivate the removal of the calcification and sediments generated by false populist certainties, towards promoting a culture of methodical research and conscious concern regarding public issues, in order to create an environment capable of producing creative, free, and independent thought.
