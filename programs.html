Our Work

Driving change through strategic and impactful programs.

Hope on the Horizon

This international youth program is designed to support and enhance the capabilities of young people in peacebuilding. It focuses on developing their leadership skills, promoting their creative initiatives, and connecting them with a global network of peers. Participants engage in workshops, mentorship, and community-based projects to become effective agents of change.

Our Activities and Programs

Our core activities revolve around three pillars: Education and Awareness, Capacity Building, and Advocacy. We conduct awareness campaigns, training sessions on non-violent communication and human rights, vocational training for economic empowerment, and advocate for policies that support youth and women's participation in decision-making processes.
